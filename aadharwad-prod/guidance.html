GUIDANCE-MOTIVATION

Stay positive, work hard, make it happen.

Our mind is a powerful thing. When we fill it with positive thoughts, our life starts to change. Therefore, we regularly organize motivational and guidance sessions for farmers as well as their children. Agricultural experts help farmers to learn and adopt recommended techniques, strategies - to make profitable farming. These motivational sessions also divert them from all negative thoughts - one of the key reason behind farmer suicides. The career guidance sessions of IAS, IPS officers and other social icons are being conducted for their children, so that they can figure out their own ways to success.
