SPREAD A SMILE

Happiness doubles when you share it with someone.

It's a fact that not everyone in this world has the fate to cherish the fullest form of happiness. Spread A Smile is a campaign led by Aadharwad Foundation to bring those happy moments for orphans, children of deceased farmers and other destitute children living on the streets. These are the children who do not get to enjoy the essence of childhood. By organizing small celebrations and other fun events, Aadharwad aims to equip these children with the means they require for a healthy future.

We strongly believe that a single act of kindness throws out roots in all directions, and the roots spring up and make new trees - that is why Spread A Smile.
