We fight together

We are humans

“Jai Jawan , Jai Kisan” - This slogan of a visionary prime minister Shri. Lal Bahadur Shastri had lost its potential over the time. After the independence, according to Gandhiji's vision of Gram-Swaraj, villages and specially farmers were to be the main focus of any development plan of India. As years passed, by agriculture as an industry lost its importance for policy makers of India. This over the time caused severe distress among the farmers leading to recent dramatic rise in the number of suicides among farmer community.

Although now there can not be a sudden stop to all this, but we strongly feel that some small but immediate actions can definitely reduce the intensity of such incidents. It is everyone's equal responsibility to help farmers. There is so much that needs to do and we cannot do all of that alone, but if everyone of us do their bit of responsibility, then it surely will lead to a big change.

Let’s fight together, to save our farmers, the soul of our country.

We care for others

We are humans

We strongly believe that if children are given good primary education and enough opportunities, they can figure out their own way out their own way to a brighter future.

It hurts deeply to realize that there are many school drop outs in rural area, especially in farmer families, in spite of the fact that seeking education is a fundamental right. While we cannot possibly solve all issues, we can at least help some of them with financing, resources and counseling.

There is so much to do in this space, but we currently focus on students from small villages in Maharashtra, doing as much as we can to enable these students to continue their schooling.

There are those that need help and there those who want to help, We act as a bridge connecting them.

Our Key Initiatives

MISSION SAVE FARMER

We support poor farmers, especially the families of deceased farmers, by furrowing their farms and providing quality seeds, fertilizer, pesticides, all at free of cost. This is to reduce the financial burden and prevent them from ending their own lives.
DONATE NOW

MEDICAL AID

We provide financial aid for farmers, old age people and other poor families, in case of any medical emergency. We also help physically challenged persons by providing them much needed supportive accessories to make their lives better.
DONATE NOW

EMERGENCY AID

We provide emergency financial assistance for farmers and other poor families in case of any natural calamities or any other unpredicted situations like dry droughts, fire tragedy, earth-quake, heavy rain fall or flood like situations.
DONATE NOW

EDUCATIONAL AID

We support education of children of deceased farmers, orphan, physically challenged and poor family kids. We provide career counselling, and also motivate them towards competitive exams. It is one of our key initiative and being managed via Kartavya.

About us

The Aadharwad Foundation is a registered society established to help poor and needy farmers families, especially in the region of Marathwada, without restriction to any cast, community or religion.

It intends to help farmers morally, financially or in any other way, in order to prevent them from committing suicide. It provides assistance for their farming and also immediate financial aid in case of medical or any other emergency. It also support education of their children and if required then provide best possible financial aid for their higher education or preparation of competitive exams.

Highlights

  • Immediate financial aid for 50 deceased farmer's families across Marathwada during 2014-15. Complete farming assistance for 10 deceased farmer's families in 2016 and again to 8 families in 2017.
  • Emergency financial assistance to 10 needy families during their medical and other emergency situations in year 2016.
  • Educational support for more than 225 students for current academic year 2017-2018.
