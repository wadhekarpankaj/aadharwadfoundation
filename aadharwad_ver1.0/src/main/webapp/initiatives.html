OUR INITIATIVES

We rise by lifting others.

MISSION SAVE FARMER

This is our prime initiative to support poor farmers, especially the families of deceased farmers, by extending immediate financial assistance and/or complete farming assistance for a crop season. This is to prevent them from ending their own lives.
Read more

MEDICAL AID

We provide financial aid for farmers, old age people and other poor families, in case of any medical emergency. We also help physically challenged persons by providing them much needed supportive accessories to make their lives better.
Read more

EMERGENCY AID

We provide emergency financial assistance for farmers and other poor families in case of any natural calamities or any other unpredicted disasters like dry droughts, fire tragedy, earth-quake, heavy rain fall or flood like situations.
Read More

SPREAD A SMILE

This initiative is to share happiness and spread smiles among all those who could do not have it. Our volunteers celebrate festivals and other happy moments with destitute children, homeless people and make them feel like one among us.
Read more

EDUCATIONAL AID

We support education of children of deceased farmers, orphan, physically challenged and poor family kids. We provide career counseling, and also motivate them towards competitive exams. It is one of our key initiative and being managed via Kartavya.
Read more

STUDY CENTER

We have established a COMPETITIVE EXAM STUDY CENTER in Partur, having all required books, material for those who preparing or MPSC or other competitive exams. Also available for other students for their general academic study purpose.
Read more

MOTIVATION & GUIDANCE

We regularly organize guidance sessions of agricultural experts to motivate farmers to learn and adopt recommended techniques, strategies. Also the career guidance and motivational sessions are being conducted for their children.
Read More

HELP THE HELPER

We support other NGOs and Groups in various help initiatives that they are planning to do or already doing. The intention is to motivate new comers to do social work and to strengthen the social workers network in the region.
